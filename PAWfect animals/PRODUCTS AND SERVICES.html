Our Products & Services

Comprehensive veterinary care for every stage of your pet's life

Our Services

We here at PAWfect Animals Practice aim at providing a full spectrum of high-quality veterinary services to keep our precious pets healthy and happy through every stage of life.

Our amazing and friendly team is dedicated to offering compassionate care tailored to your pet's unique needs.

Core Medical Services We Provide

Preventive Care and Wellness

It is extremely important that our pets get the vaccinations/preventions that they need, and because of that, we offer:

  • Routine health examinations for puppies/kittens, adults, and seniors
  • Vaccination programs - core and lifestyle based
  • Parasite prevention and control for fleas, ticks, heartworm, intestinal parasites
  • Microchipping and registration
  • Nutritional counselling and weight management
  • Behavioral consultations

Diagnostic Services

We provide a wide range of medical tests, imaging scans, and procedures that help pet owners determine the nature and severity of their pet's illness.

We offer:

  • In-house laboratory testing - bloodwork, urinalysis, fecal tests
  • Digital Radiology (X-rays) - for faster results
  • Ultrasound imaging
  • Electrocardiography (ECG/EKG) for heart health
  • Blood pressure monitoring

Surgical Services

This is to offer comprehensive care from diagnosis to post-operative support.

  • Spay and neuter procedures
  • Soft tissue surgery (e.g. mass removals, abdominal surgery)
  • Orthopedic surgery
  • Dental surgery
  • Pre-surgical bloodwork, dedicated surgical monitoring, advanced anesthesia protocols, and comprehensive pain management

Dental Care

It is highly important for animals to have good oral hygiene because without good oral hygiene, germs can reach levels that might lead to infections, such as tooth decay and gum disease.

We provide dental care as well as oral hygiene:

  • Dental exams and cleaning
  • Digital dental X-ray
  • Tooth extractions and oral surgery
  • Home dental care education and products

Specialized and Additional Services

We also have the following for convenience:

Pharmacy

We offer a well-stocked pharmacy for convenient access to medication, prescription diets, and preventative products.

Dermatology

For the diagnosis and treatment of skin allergies and ear infections.

Chronic Disease Management

For ongoing care for conditions like diabetes, kidney disease, arthritis, or thyroid issues.

Geriatric (Senior Pet) Care

For specific wellness plans for older pets, including pain management and mobility support.

Puppy and Kitten Care

For special packages for new pets (first exams, vaccines, socialization tips).

End-of-Life Care

For humane euthanasia and pet loss support/grief resources.

House Calls

We also offer house calls for your convenience.

Mobile Services

To come to your local parks for easy access to veterinary care.

What is PAWfect Animals Practice all about?

We are a fully functional full-service veterinary practice that is aimed to serve the pet owners of Soweto.

Our number 1 mission is to provide compassionate and exceptional medical care for your fluffy buddies (pets) while building a lasting and honest relationship with the owners.

We believe in treating every animal as if they were our own and in building a genuine, lifelong partnership with pet lovers/parents.

How Did We Come to Be?

Our practice was founded in 2022 by Dr K Mokoena, Dr M Mbele, Dr M Kgoadi and Dr L Masina, and it all started with a simple dream: to create a veterinary practice that will make both animals and animal owners feel welcome, safe, cared for, and understood.

What began as a dream in a small two room building has grown into a full animal practice, thanks to the trust of our amazing community.

"Our passion for animals drives everything we do. We're not just treating pets; we're caring for family members."
- The PAWfect Animals Founders
